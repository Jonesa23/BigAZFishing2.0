Welcome to BigAZFishing!

Learn to Fish the Arizona Waters

We welcome everyone to BigAZFishing, where you can learn the ins and outs of fishing the waters here in Arizona! AZ has some of the best fishing in the country, as fishing can be fun all-year round, no matter the time of year! Learn the basics of fishing, the different fishing locations here in AZ, and the species of fish that lurk in the Arizona waters! All of your Arizona fishing inquiries can be found on this website, in BigAZFishing!

Robert - February 5, 2025

We were over at Luna Lake in Alpine yesterday, shorefishing for some trout. We were skunked all evening, we tried all kinds of baits, spinning lures, these darn trout weren't biting anything! I told my son Ledoux "one more cast", because it was getting awfully dark, the sun had already set. On his last cast, a trout bit onto the lure, and boy reeled in that sucker!

Teemothy - February 12, 2025

I was fishing on the Verde River this morning, the bass were really loving the fluke baits. This was my first catch of the day, just a lil' guy, but he straight up inhaled that bait! That was a rough one to take out of his mouth, it was literally in his stomach! Good news, the little guy was safely returned to the waters!
