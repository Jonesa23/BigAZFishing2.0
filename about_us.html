About Us!

Fellow Arizona native Jones Allison started this webpage in order to help all anglers locally from Arizona and even outsiders fish in this state. Hailing from Mesa, Arizona, Jones Allison grew an unconditional love for the sport of fishing when he was very young, often fishing the local canals with his father during his adolescence. Jones has also experienced fishing outside of Arizona, some of his favorite places he has fished at are Alaska, Idaho, and the Florida Keys.

Our Mission!

Our mission is simply to help all anglers learn something new and to increase their love and passion for fishing in Arizona. The content in this webpage is mainly dedicated to those who are new to fishing, but even fishing veterans can learn a thing or two from this webpage! At first, fishing in Arizona may seem discouraging, and you might even get skunked some days. And for that reason we have created this webpage, to help Arizona anglers become prepared to tackle the waters and catch some fishies every dang time!
